How to Play Shithead

  • Goal: Be the first to get rid of all your cards. The last player with cards is the "shithead".
  • Setup: Each player gets 3 cards face-down, 3 face-up on top, and 3 in hand.
  • Playing: Play cards equal to or higher than the top card.
  • Special Cards:
    • 2: Resets the pile (can be played on anything)
    • 3: Invisible card - can be played on anything, affects next player
    • 7: Next player must play 7 or lower
    • 10: Burns the entire pile
    • üÉè Joker: Flips the direction of play (clockwise ‚Üî counter-clockwise)
  • Order: Play cards from hand first, then face-up cards, then face-down cards (blind).
  • Picking up: If you can't play, pick up the entire discard pile.
  • Winning: First to clear all cards wins!
